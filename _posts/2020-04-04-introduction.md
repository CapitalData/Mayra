Mayra obtained her Bachelors of Science in Human Development and Family Sciences at the University of Texas at Austin. Before graduating she was a practicum student at AGE of Central Texas where she worked with elderly patients and their caregivers affected by Alzheimer's and other forms of dementia.

In her spare time she enjoys spending time outdoors with her children,  visiting friends and family, and exploring her city and surrounding areas.
